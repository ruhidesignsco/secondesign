Our Story

The Heart Behind Ruhi

Two sisters. One shared dream. A love for Arabic calligraphy.

A Dream Shared Between Sisters

Ruhi began during a holiday break, when two sisters found themselves with free time and a shared desire to do something meaningful with it. Instead of letting the days pass idly, they decided to channel their energy into something creative — something that combined their love for art with the beauty of their culture.

Both had always been artistic at heart, and what started as a way to make the most of their time quickly grew into a passion project. They picked up punch needles, experimented with Arabic calligraphy, and discovered a craft that felt like it was made for them. Each piece they created carried a word with weight — faith, patience, love, gratitude — stitched with intention and care.

What began as a holiday hobby became a full-blown ambition. The sisters realised they weren't just making art — they were building something of their own. Ruhi Designs was born from that spark: two sisters, a shared dream, and the belief that beautiful things happen when you invest your time in what you love.

Every piece we create is stitched by hand using the punch needle technique, blending traditional calligraphy with modern artistry. We pour our hearts into every thread, every curve of every letter, because for us, Ruhi is more than a business — it's our soul's work.

— The Ruhi Sisters
